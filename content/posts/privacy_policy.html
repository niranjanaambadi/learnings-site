Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Lawmate ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Lawmate Case Sync Chrome Extension. This policy explains how we handle user data in compliance with the Google Chrome Web Store User Data Policy.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Single Purpose: The extension exists solely to help advocates synchronize case data from official legal portals to their Lawmate dashboard.
  • Website Content: We collect case numbers, party names, and filing statuses only from the Kerala High Court e-filing portal (efiling.highcourtofkerala.nic.in).
  • Authentication Information: We store session tokens to maintain your secure connection to api.lawmate.in. We do not store your plain-text passwords.
  • Log Data: Standard IP address logging occurs during API requests for security and rate-limiting purposes.

2. Data Protection & Limited Use

We strictly adhere to the Google Business Model Policy:

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use or transfer your data for serving ads, including retargeting, personalized, or interest-based advertising.
  • We do not use your data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

3. Your Consent

By using Lawmate Case Sync, you consent to the collection of case-related metadata as described for the purpose of case management. We do not track your browsing history on any websites outside of our declared host permissions.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@lawmate.in.
